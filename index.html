Limca Book of Records.

The Limca Book of Records (LBR) is an annual publication that documents world records held by Indians. With over 30 editions since 1987.

It is India's first and longest continuously published book of records. The LBR catalogues unique milestones and landmarks including, but not limited to, firsts, bests, mosts, inventions, discoveries, and honours and awards received, by Indians at home or abroad in all fields – both human and natural.
